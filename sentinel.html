Sentinel Aviation
Sentinel was a project that I worked on as a part of a team at The Rothwell Group. I wrote the SASS, HTML5 and Angular as the senior front end developer/designer. We created a service for individuals to book private jets from their personal devices. in addition, several dashboards and CMS were constructed for operators, pilots, and client facing users.
Since this project has not yet been released, it is still under a NDA. Please feel free to ask me any further questions you may have.
